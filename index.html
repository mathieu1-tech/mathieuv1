Mathieu Konstruct Solutions Co. Ltd. Prefabricated Modular Units

Mathieu Konstruct Solutions Co. Ltd. provides prefabricated modular units, container houses, construction, engineering, design, and customized prefab solutions.

Products

  • Engineering Container House - for medium to large-scale engineering projects, site offices, staff housing, and scalable construction facilities.
  • Customized Container House - custom layouts, color options, wall panels, and expandable prefab configurations.
  • 1-Day Quick Build Houses - rapid housing and temporary facilities with reduced on-site work.
  • A-Frame House - vacation homes, resorts, glamping, Airbnb stays, and leisure developments.
  • Space Capsule House - living, working, leisure, resort attractions, and premium modular guest units.

Common Questions

Units can be customized by layout, color, panels, and optional components. Common applications include site offices, temporary housing, resorts, commercial kiosks, glamping, and vacation units. Pricing is prepared after receiving product type, quantity, and project location.
